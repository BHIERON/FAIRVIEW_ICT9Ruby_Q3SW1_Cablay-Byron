MODERN FAMILY

Jay Pritchett, a businessman, and his children, Claire and Mitchell, connect three distinct Los Angeles-area families in Modern Family.

Fulgencio Joseph "Joe" Pritchett and Manuel "Manny" Delgado, a son from Gloria's previous marriage, were born to Jay and Gloria Delgado. Javier, Gloria's ex-husband, and DeDe, Jay's ex-wife occasionally appear on the program.

Claire Dunphy is Jay's daughter and CEO of Pritchett's Closets and Blinds. She is married to Phil Dunphy, a real estate agent and "cool dad". They have three kids: Luke, Alex, and Haley. Dylan, Haley's on-again, off-again lover, is a constant on the show. They are married at the end of the tenth season.

Mitchell and Cameron Tucker adopted their daughter Lily Tucker-Pritchett, a Vietnamese. In the series finale, they add Rexford to their family.

The episodes are based on real-life events, depicting a modern family.


the dunphies

philip humphrey "phil" dunphy

Realtor Philip Humphrey "Phil" Dunphy works in real estate. He is Claire's spouse and the father to Haley, Alex, and Luke. He treats his son more like a buddy than a father and has a youthful mindset.

Claire Melinda Dunphy

Former housewife and stay-at-home mother Claire Melinda Dunphy is currently employed by her father's closet business. She was previously an account manager at Starcrest Hotels. She was out of control and crazy as a teenager. She is the mother of Haley, Alex, and Luke and the wife of Phil Dunphy.

Haley Gwendolyn

The oldest Dunphy kid is Haley Gwendolyn. She is a fashion designer as well as a college student. She is referred regarded as the Dunphy student who prefers partying and hanging out with friends than studying. Due to the fact that Haley is a teenager much like Claire, Claire is concerned about her safety. She quickly leaves for college but is later expelled due to underage drinking, refusing arrest, and unintentional attack on a police officer. She starts at a different university and is hired as a fashion designer. She had a sporadic connection with Dylan and is presently seeing Andy Bailey, Jay and Gloria's male nanny.

Alexandria Anastasia "Alex" Dunphy

The middle child of the Dunphy family is Alexandria Anastasia "Alex" Dunphy. She is Haley's polar opposite since she prioritizes her academics over social events, gatherings with friends, and other social activities.

Lucas Philip "Luke" Dunphy

The sole son and youngest child of the Dunphy family is Lucas Philip "Luke" Dunphy. He has a reputation for being rather dim and boisterous. He seldom gets into arguments with his sisters and is pretty innocent. Before Season 5, when he seems to start showing interest, he has never been interested in having a girlfriend.

the prichetts


Jason Francis "Jay" Pritchett

Jason Francis "Jay" Pritchett, is the family patriarch and the richest member of the group. He has been in charge for many years and owns a prosperous closet business. Jay owns a sizable home with a sizable yard and a pool.

The little son of Jay and Gloria Pritchett is Fulgencio Joseph "Joe" Pritchett. He is a charming, bouncy youngster who was rather aggressive when he was born in "Party Crasher." In Season 7, he starts to speak more.
